About Us

PT. Kastara Group Indonesia

PT Kastara Group Indonesia it is a business entity that is engaged in agricultural sector. Currently, our company distributed Indonesian commodity to our domestic and global market. We have several kinds of commodity products such Cashew Nuts, Coffee Beans, tea, Vanilla beans, soybeans and coconut charcoal. Our firm was build in early 2022 and are also supported by the experience farmer on their sector (that are can be responsible for its credibility based on the local government) to processing our best product to be completed as our client request as well. The purpose of our company is develop and prospering our local farmers to maximize the potential agricultural for the future. In the future, we will do development for our marketing side to provide another product such as spices and herbal to reach out wider export market goals.

Ex-works

Free on Board (FOB)

We responsible for delivering goods to a designated port and loading them onto a vessel through forwarder with the buyer assuming responsibility for transportation costs and risks thereafter.

Cost and Freight (CNF)

We responsible to delivering goods from designated to destination port including the cost of of the goods and the freight charges. Once the goods are loaded onto the vessel, the risk shifts from the seller to the buyer.

Cost, Insurance and Freight (CIF)
